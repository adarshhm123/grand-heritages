Welcome to Grand Heritage!

Welcome to Hotel Heritage, the ideal Mumbai accommodation for both business and vacation travelers. Our excellent location, just 4 minutes from Kurla Railway Station and 10 minutes from BKC makes it simple to reach important locations. For individuals who enjoy shopping, the nearby Phoenix Market City Mall is an ideal destination for avid shoppers. Come into our inviting and friendly space, where we take great delight in offering our esteemed visitors outstanding customer service. Our spacious accommodations are thoughtfully designed for budget-conscious travelers in Mumbai. Additionally, the U.S. Embassy is conveniently located just 3.4 km away. Enjoy the convenience of round-the-clock front desk assistance and room service throughout your stay.
